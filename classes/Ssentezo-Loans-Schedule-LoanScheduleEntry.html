LoanScheduleEntry
in package
  • Application

LoanScheduleEntry.php : 17

This is a single line in the loan schedule.

It has all the information you may ever need about the line in the schedule and can be used to answer questions like

  • How much of principal is due for this repayment, installement or just the debt
  • How many repayments are left, and how much should be paid.
  • etc
