ActiveRepayments extends Repayments
in package
  • Application

ActiveRepayments.php : 16

Active repayments are repaymnets which have some unused money The unused money can later be used.

This class gives you a mechanism of interacting with such repayments and be in position to perfom the following:

  • Remove money
  • Check balance
  • count them
  • access all of the them
  • revert / take back the money
